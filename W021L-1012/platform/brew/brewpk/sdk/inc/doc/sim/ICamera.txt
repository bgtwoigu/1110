Simulator Support DOCUMENTATION

ICamera simulation

ICamera is partly simulated.

It is simulated when Directshow compliant camera is connected
and Devicepack with "Camera Support" (IDS_DD_CAMERA_SUPP) as yes 
and Camera CLSID support(IDS_AEECLSID_CAMERA) as yes is chosen. 

Camera simulator support can be restricted if Directshow compliant
camera doesn't support any of the following BREW Simulator
simulated features:

- EncodeSnapshot simulated for BMP format only.

- RecordMovie simulated for 3G2 and MPG4 formats only.

Only SQCIF, QCIF and QVGA movie sizes simulated.

- Snapshot encoding and movie recording simulated for
MMD_FILE_NAME destination only.

===h2>
